WAVingS

This is a game that you have to avoid falling down on the ocean.
The more distance you walk through, the more score you get.
Beware: the game will get harder and harder!!!

Control:
W, K, ↑ : moving up
S, J, ↓ : moving down
A, H, ← : moving left
D, L, → : moving right

Posted by Victor Lee, Aoi, Guan, & WEI-CHENG HUNG on March 18, 2018

Friendly Dungeon

You woke up and found yourself locked in a dungeon.
There are coins around you, but picking them up slows you down.
You can also trade coins for keys, which open doors.
Beware: the dungeon can turn wild.

Control:
W, ↑ : moving up
S, ↓ : moving down
A, ← : moving left
D, → : moving right

Posted by Victor Lee, & Dawn on December 5, 2017

Across the Ancient

You have to find a way to escape the island.
Finally, you will find the island is not just a desert island you thought before.

Control:
↑ : moving up
↓ : moving down
← : moving left
→ : moving right
Q : changing to left tool
E: changing to right tool
